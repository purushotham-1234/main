Cobra

The cobra is a venomous snake famous for its hood and defensive posture.

Key Characteristics:

  • Venomous with neurotoxic venom
  • Expandable hood when threatened
  • Can grow up to 18 feet in length
  • Found in Asia and Africa

Interesting Facts:

  • Cobras “stand up” and spread their hood to appear bigger to predators.
  • The King Cobra is the longest venomous snake in the world.
  • They can “spit” venom to defend against attackers.
